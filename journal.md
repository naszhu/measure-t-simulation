  
List 

1. **Psychological Science** – This is one of the leading journals in cognitive psychology, covering a broad range of topics within psychology, including experimental research, theory, and methodological innovations.

2. **Cognition** – A highly respected journal focusing on cognitive science, with articles that span a range of topics from perception and memory to decision-making, language, and computational models.

3. **Journal of Experimental Psychology: General** – This journal publishes cutting-edge research on cognition, memory, perception, learning, and other psychological processes.

4. **Cognitive Psychology** – Another top journal that specifically focuses on experimental studies of cognitive processes, such as attention, memory, and decision-making.

5. **Trends in Cognitive Sciences (TICS)** – A journal that focuses on cutting-edge developments in cognitive science and is widely read in the field, though it’s more review-based compared to experimental research journals.

6. **Journal of Cognitive Neuroscience (JCN)** – A journal that bridges the gap between cognitive psychology and neuroscience, frequently publishing research on the neural basis of cognitive functions.

7. **Cognitive Science** – A multidisciplinary journal for cognitive science, including work on computational models, cognitive psychology, linguistics, and artificial intelligence.
