## VERB STRENGTH REFERENCE LISTS

### **STRONG VERBS - For Strong Evidence**
**When to use:** Meta-analyses, systematic reviews, multiple replications with consistent results, decades of research

| Verb | Usage Note |
|------|------------|
| **demonstrates** | Shows clearly with strong evidence |
| **establishes** | Proves or sets up as fact |
| **shows** | Reveals with clear evidence |
| **confirms** | Verifies with certainty |
| **reveals** | Makes evident through research |
| **proves*** | *Use sparingly; prefer "demonstrates" or "establishes" |

---

### **MODERATE VERBS - For Moderate Evidence**
**When to use:** Single solid study, 2-3 studies with consistent findings, reasonable evidence, correlational data

| Verb | Usage Note |
|------|------------|
| **suggests** | Indicates but doesn't prove |
| **indicates** | Points to a conclusion |
| **supports** | Provides evidence for |
| **proposes** | Puts forward a claim |
| **implies** | Suggests indirectly |
| **points to** | Directs toward a conclusion |

---

### **WEAK VERBS - For Weak Evidence**
**When to use:** Preliminary findings, exploratory studies, theoretical speculation, single-case studies, small samples

| Verb | Usage Note |
|------|------------|
| **may** | Possibility, not certainty |
| **might** | Weaker possibility |
| **could** | Theoretical possibility |
| **appears to** | Seems but not confirmed |
| **seems to** | Gives impression of |
| **tentatively suggests** | Very cautious indication |

## ASSIGNMENT 1: Paragraph Correction

**Instructions:** The following paragraph contains multiple collocation and verb strength errors. Rewrite the entire paragraph with all corrections. 

### **Messy Paragraph:**

Recent investigations have made research on the relationship between working memory capacity and academic achievement. The research team did three experiments with undergraduate students and gathered data from over 300 participants. After they examined the results, they found important findings that helped their hypothesis about individual differences. A meta-analysis of 15 studies might suggest that working memory training can improve academic performance, though there is opposing evidence from some early studies. The researchers created a theory framework to explain the basic mechanism by which working memory affects learning outcomes. Their results are consistent to previous research and give light on cognitive processes underlying academic success. However, critics question the assumptions of this model and argue that one preliminary study with 20 participants demonstrates a causal relationship is premature. The team suggests a hypothesis that executive function, rather than raw capacity, is the basic principle determining performance. They took a mixed-methods approach and used advanced neuroimaging to explain variance in achievement scores. The computational model proves that working memory limitations account for 40% of individual differences, though this interpretation fits with only some existing theories. Overall, the strong results support an emerging consensus, but the initial findings didn't show clear boundaries between different cognitive constructs.

## ASSIGNMENT 2: Original Writing Task

**Instructions:** Write a 250-300 word paragraph 

### **Research Scenario:**

You are a cognitive psychology researcher writing the discussion section of a paper. Here is your study information:

**Your Study:**
- You conducted a 6-month longitudinal study on sleep and memory consolidation
- Sample: 85 participants (moderate sample size)
- Method: Mixed-methods approach combining behavioral testing and sleep diary data
- You collected data weekly and analyzed it using multilevel modeling
- Results: Sleep quality (not just duration) was associated with better memory performance

**Related Literature:**
- Meta-analysis of 23 studies (Jones et al., 2023) shows strong relationship between sleep and memory
- Two well-controlled experiments (Smith, 2022; Lee, 2023) found similar patterns
- One preliminary study with 12 participants (Chen, 2021) had contradictory findings
- A computational model (Garcia, 2024) proposes that consolidation happens during REM sleep specifically
- Eight independent replications across different laboratories established that sleep deprivation impairs encoding

**Your Task:** Write a discussion paragraph that:
1. Summarizes what your study found
2. Relates your findings to the existing literature (mentioned above)
3. Mentions the theoretical framework
4. Acknowledges the contradictory evidence
5. Discusses the underlying mechanism
