# Mandarin Chinese Learning Syllabus - Vishnu
## 中文学习课程大纲

*Building Fluency Through Structured Learning*  
*通过结构化学习建立流利度*

---

## Course Overview
**Student:** Vishnu  
**Focus:** Mandarin Chinese Language Acquisition  
**Duration:** 14 weeks per level (3 hours/week)  
**Target:** Progressive mastery from Beginner to Advanced

---

## Level 1: Beginner (A1–A2)
**Duration:** 14 weeks (3 hrs/week)  
**Goal:** Build foundation in pronunciation, basic grammar, and daily communication

| Week | Theme / Topic        | Grammar Focus            | Vocabulary Focus      | Skills & Tasks             |
| :--- | :------------------- | :----------------------- | :-------------------- | :------------------------- |
| 1    | Pinyin & tones       | Sentence structure (SVO) | Greetings, numbers    | Tone drills, self-intro    |
| 2    | Family & nationality | 是 / 不 / 吗                | Family, countries     | Talk about family          |
| 3    | Daily life           | 有 / 在 / 没有               | Time, places          | Describe routine           |
| 4    | Food & restaurants   | 想 / 要 / 点菜               | Food, drinks          | Order food                 |
| 5    | Shopping             | Measure words            | Clothes, prices       | Bargain dialogue           |
| 6    | Weather & seasons    | 正在 / 会                   | Weather, activities   | Describe weather plans     |
| 7    | Transportation       | 去 / 从 / 到                | Directions, transport | Give directions            |
| 8    | School & work        | 可以 / 不能                  | Professions           | Talk about schedule        |
| 9    | Leisure & hobbies    | 喜欢 / 最                   | Hobbies, sports       | Express likes              |
| 10   | Review & mid-term    | –                        | –                     | Listening/speaking test    |
| 11   | Travel               | 过 / 去过                   | Cities, sights        | Talk about trips           |
| 12   | Health               | 要 / 得 / 应该               | Body, illness         | Roleplay doctor visit      |
| 13   | Holidays & culture   | 因为…所以…                   | Festivals             | Share festival habits      |
| 14   | Final project        | –                        | –                     | Short oral + writing piece |

### **Level 1 Learning Objectives:**
By the end of Level 1, Vishnu will be able to:
- Master Pinyin pronunciation and four tones
- Use basic sentence structures (SVO) correctly
- Introduce oneself and talk about family
- Order food and shop using basic phrases
- Describe daily routines and preferences
- Give simple directions and talk about transportation
- Express likes, dislikes, and basic opinions
- Handle simple travel and health situations
- Write short paragraphs (50-100 characters)

### **Level 1 Assessment:**
- **Weekly:** Vocabulary quizzes, speaking practice
- **Mid-term (Week 10):** Listening comprehension + oral presentation
- **Final (Week 14):** Integrated test (listening, speaking, reading, writing)

## Level 2: Intermediate (B1–B2)
**Duration:** 14 weeks (3 hrs/week)  
**Goal:** Develop fluency in complex conversations and written expression

| Week | Theme                   | Grammar Focus       | Vocabulary      | Skills & Tasks          |
| :--- | :---------------------- | :------------------ | :-------------- | :---------------------- |
| 1    | Review & storytelling   | 然后 / 后来 / 以前        | Time, sequence  | Narrate a memory        |
| 2    | Opinions & reasons      | 虽然…但是…              | Feelings, logic | Give opinions           |
| 3    | Social life             | 跟 / 对 / 给           | Relationships   | Write a message         |
| 4    | Describing experiences  | 了 / 过 / 着           | Travel, events  | Oral recount            |
| 5    | Comparing & contrasting | 比 / 更 / 最           | Adjectives      | Compare two things      |
| 6    | City & environment      | 如果 / 就 / 要是         | Nature, society | Discuss environment     |
| 7    | Culture & art           | 把 / 被               | Art, customs    | Interpret a story       |
| 8    | Work & goals            | 得 / 不得 / 让          | Jobs, study     | Talk about ambition     |
| 9    | Emotion & reflection    | 一边…一边…              | Emotions        | Write diary entries     |
| 10   | Storytelling techniques | 连词 usage            | Narrative verbs | Build story plot        |
| 11   | Technology & media      | Passive forms       | Internet terms  | Debate on media         |
| 12   | Review & writing        | Advanced connectors | Linking words   | Write 200-word essay    |
| 13   | Short story project     | –                   | –               | Story presentation      |
| 14   | Final assessment        | –                   | –               | Speaking + writing test |

### **Level 2 Learning Objectives:**
By the end of Level 2, Vishnu will be able to:
- Narrate past experiences and tell stories coherently
- Express opinions with supporting reasons
- Use complex grammar structures (把/被, 虽然…但是…)
- Compare and contrast ideas effectively
- Discuss abstract topics (culture, environment, technology)
- Write structured essays (200+ characters)
- Participate in debates and discussions
- Understand intermediate-level texts and audio

### **Level 2 Assessment:**
- **Weekly:** Grammar exercises, conversation practice
- **Mid-term (Week 7):** Storytelling presentation
- **Final (Week 14):** Essay writing + oral debate

## Level 3: Advanced (C1)
**Duration:** 14–16 weeks (3 hrs/week)  
**Goal:** Achieve near-native fluency and cultural competence

| Week | Focus                     | Grammar / Style | Vocabulary & Skills  | Tasks                         |
| :--- | :------------------------ | :-------------- | :------------------- | :---------------------------- |
| 1    | Tone refinement & idioms  | 成语 / 比喻         | Idioms & metaphors   | Story analysis                |
| 2    | Essay structure           | 段落衔接 / 转折       | Transitional phrases | Write formal essay            |
| 3    | Cultural discourse        | 假如 / 尽管         | Debate language      | Debate: culture vs. modernity |
| 4    | Storytelling mastery      | 高级描写结构          | Descriptive verbs    | Write a fable                 |
| 5    | Argument & persuasion     | 因此 / 由此可见       | Argument connectors  | Opinion article               |
| 6    | Classical references      | 文言色彩            | Cultural idioms      | Analyze proverb origin        |
| 7    | Abstract discussion       | 被动+主动态混用        | Abstract vocabulary  | Discuss philosophy            |
| 8    | Review & writing workshop | –               | –                    | Draft a reflective essay      |
| 9    | Speech & presentation     | 修辞 / 比喻         | Oral tone, flow      | Oral storytelling             |
| 10   | Final project             | –               | –                    | 3–5 min oral + 400-word story |

### **Level 3 Learning Objectives:**
By the end of Level 3, Vishnu will be able to:
- Use Chinese idioms (成语) and metaphors naturally
- Write formal essays with sophisticated structure
- Engage in advanced cultural and philosophical discussions
- Understand classical Chinese references
- Deliver presentations with rhetorical techniques
- Write creative stories (400+ characters)
- Analyze complex texts and media
- Express nuanced opinions on abstract topics

### **Level 3 Assessment:**
- **Weekly:** Advanced reading comprehension, idiom usage
- **Mid-term (Week 7):** Philosophical discussion
- **Final (Week 10):** Comprehensive project (oral + written)

---

## Progressive Skill Building Across All Levels

### **Speaking Progression:**
- **Level 1:** Basic phrases, simple Q&A
- **Level 2:** Extended conversations, storytelling
- **Level 3:** Debates, presentations, nuanced expression

### **Listening Progression:**
- **Level 1:** Slow, clear speech on familiar topics
- **Level 2:** Natural speed conversations, various accents
- **Level 3:** Academic lectures, media, rapid speech

### **Reading Progression:**
- **Level 1:** Short texts with Pinyin, simple stories
- **Level 2:** News articles, intermediate literature
- **Level 3:** Academic papers, classical references, complex texts

### **Writing Progression:**
- **Level 1:** Simple sentences, short paragraphs (50-100 chars)
- **Level 2:** Structured essays, stories (200+ chars)
- **Level 3:** Formal essays, creative writing (400+ chars)

---

## Weekly Practice Structure

### **Daily Practice (30-45 minutes)**
- **Monday:** Vocabulary review + new words
- **Tuesday:** Grammar exercises
- **Wednesday:** Listening practice (podcasts/videos)
- **Thursday:** Reading comprehension
- **Friday:** Speaking practice (conversation/recording)
- **Weekend:** Writing practice + review

### **Weekly Class Activities (3 hours)**
- **Warm-up:** Review previous lesson (15 min)
- **Main Lesson:** New grammar + vocabulary (60 min)
- **Practice:** Interactive exercises (45 min)
- **Application:** Real-world scenarios (30 min)
- **Wrap-up:** Homework assignment + Q&A (30 min)

---

## Assessment Methods

### **Formative Assessment (Ongoing)**

- Writing samples

### **Summative Assessment (End of Each Level)**

## Learning Objectives Summary

By completing all three levels, Vishnu will be able to:

1. **Communicate fluently** in daily, academic, and professional contexts
2. **Read and understand** Chinese texts from news to literature
3. **Write effectively** in various genres (essays, stories, reports)
4. **Understand cultural nuances** and use appropriate language
5. **Think critically** in Chinese about complex topics
6. **Continue learning independently** with advanced study strategies
